üìù How to Flash

Step 1: Connect Your Board

Connect your ESP32/ESP8266 to your computer using a USB cable.

Step 2: Select Firmware

Choose the firmware variant that matches your hardware setup above.

Step 3: Click Install

Click the "Install" button and select the serial port when prompted.

Step 4: Wait for Completion

The firmware will be flashed automatically. This takes 1-2 minutes.

Step 5: Setup Your Device

After flashing, connect to WiFi network "mypvlog-XXXX" and open http://192.168.4.1 to configure.
